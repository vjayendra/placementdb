INSTRUCTIONS for STEP 1
Creating Placement Email-Id



  • Format = firstname.usn@gmail.com

    Example
    Name: Christiano Ronaldo,    USN : 1rn14cs012
    Email-Id = christiano.1rn14cs012@gmail.com

    Example
    Name: Shreyas S.R (or S.R Shreyas)    USN : 1rn13cs011
    Email-Id = shreyas.1rn13cs011@gmail.com

    (You are only required to use your FIRST-NAME in the Email-Id)

  • Only LOWERCASE letters should be used in the Email-Id.

    Example:
    christiano.1rn14cs012@gmail.com  ✔
    christiano.1RN13CS012@gmail.com  ✘
    Christiano.1RN13CS012@gmail.com  ✘
    CHRISTIANO.1RN13CS012@gmail.com  ✘

  • Make sure that the Mobile No. and the Recovery Email-Id that you associate with this Email-Id are used by you regularly.

    Messages in the future will be sent to this Mobile No. only &
    You may require to use the recovery Email in future if you forget the password for your Placement Email-Id.

  • Those who still haven't got their USN need not worry! (1st Year / Diploma or Lateral entry students or any other.) The Deadline date doesn't apply to you. Once you get your permanent USN then you can create this ID.

INSTRUCTIONS for STEP-2
Filling the "PERSONAL-INFO" form



  • Select your Branch from the Drop-Down Menu to go your branch-specific form.

  • This form is designed to accept only 1 entry from each student.

  • When you click the button, you are directed to Gmail-SignIn page.
    If you are not getting a Sign In page and are directly sent to the form, check for previous Gmail Logins.
    You might be logged into Google with some other Email Id.
    Log out of that Email Id and sign in again with your Placement Email Id.

  • If you are directed to Gmail SignIn at the first try, then
    Sign In there using your PLACEMENT EMAIL-ID Only.
    Once you are signed-in, you will be directed to the Form.
    (This SignIn is required so that you can update/correct the form in future.)

  • Make sure you are filling the form assigned to your branch only.
    Check the FORM TITLE for confirmation.

  • Once you get the to the form fill it using UPPERCASE-LETTERS only (Except Email-Ids).

  • Enter your PLACEMENT EMAIL-ID only in the box given in the form.

  • Enter your name in the boxes respectively as FIRST-MIDDLE-LAST NAME properly.

  • Do not use salutations such as MR. , MRS. , DR. , MISS. , PROF. e.t.c. in the FATHER's/MOTHER's NAME fields.
