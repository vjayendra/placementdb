INSTRUCTIONS for STEP-3
Filling the "CONNECT- DRIVE" form



What is RNSIT Connect-Drive ?


  • An initiative by RNSIT's Placement-Cell to connect more and more potential recruiters with the college.

What is a REFERENCE ?


  • A REFERENCE here is a link that creates a possibility of creating more corporate/company connections with RNSIT.
    In simple words, it will help the college's placement cell to get more job-opportunities and development scenarios in the college.


How does it all work ?


  • Suppose, your father or mother is the Vice-President of a well established XYZ-Company.
    Now, you provide your father's/mother's details in the form, using which the RNSIT Placement-Cell formally contacts the XYZ-Company with your father/mother as the "REFERENCE" under the RNSIT Connect-Drive.
    Then what ?
    Congratulations! You might then see the XYZ-Company in the next round of Placement-Drives in the college.
    Meaning more placement opportunities for you!

  • The REFERENCE person need not be your parents only.
    They can be your Elder Siblings, Uncles/Aunts, Friends, Relatives, Friend of Parents, People in your network, e.t.c.
    Also it need not only be an MNC or a Big Corporate Company that your relatives are into. I can be a Start-Up, a Self-Owned Industry/Manufacturing Company, a Business, anything!


  • Some possible EXAMPLES of REFERENCES
    • Elder brother working in an MNC.
    • Uncle working as Software Engineer in companies like Wipro, Amazon e.t.c .
    • Father has his own Business Enterprise.
    • Sister who is an Entrepreneur and has her own Start-Up.
    • Father's friend who is the C.E.O of a Construction-Company.
    • Brother who is the Manager of an Automation or Automotive company.
    • Many other such possibilities. Hope you got the idea!


Now, How do you fill the form ?


  • First of all, OPEN THIS PAGE IN A NEW INCOGNITO/PRIVATE BROWSER WINDOW, then continue reading the instructions.

  • The initial steps are similar to what you have done in "STEP 2".

  • Select your Branch from the Drop-Down Menu to go your branch-specific form.

  • This is the same form you have filled in "Step 2", A new section of "REFERENCES" has been added to it.

  • When you Login to fill the form, (it will say there that you have already filled this form, but)
    Click on "Edit your response" to go to the form.

  • The details you have entered previously will already be there, Click on "Next" to go to the "REFERENCES" section.

  • Now, about the entries in the form :

    • Say your father is an Asst. Software Engineer in TCS.
      then, in the form you fill it as follows:

      FATHER'S OCCUPATION : ENGINEER
      FATHER'S WORKPLACE/COMPANY/ORGANIZATION : TCS
      FATHER'S DESIGNATION : ASST. SOFTWARE ENGINEER

    • Say your Elder Sister is the Marketing Manager of XYZ-Company
      then, in the form you fill it as follows:

      Other Relatives/Friends who might be potential REFERENCES : ELDER SISTER
      NAME of the above mentioned REFERENCE : ELIZA OLSEN
      OCCUPATION of the REFERENCE : MANAGER
      WORKPLACE/COMPANY/ORGANIZATION of the REFERENCE : XYZ-COMPANY
      DESIGNATION of the REFERENCE : MARKETING MANAGER


    • About the "Final Declaration" Section:
      Make sure you ask the person whom you are mentioning as the "REFERENCE" whether he/she is willing to participate in the RNSIT CONNECT-DRIVE or not.
      Choose your option accordingly.

  • Please pay attention to the "DESIGNATION" fields. Make sure you enter the proper and correct designation of the REFERENCE.


  • Also read the field descriptions in the form fields for examples/clarifications.
